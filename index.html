Ubuntu Pathways

Ubuntu Pathways (formerly Ubuntu Education Fund) is a nonprofit organization that provides an integrated support system of health, education, and social support in the townships of Port Elizabeth, South Africa.
Over the past 20 years, what began as a fortuitous meeting between two strangers has grown into an internationally-recognized blueprint for community transformation.
Ubuntu has redefined mainstream development models by focusing on the depth rather than breadth of our impact on a community of 400,000.
I worked as a developer in this amazing NGO thanks to the internship in the Pier Program at the Apple Developer Academy.
My team and I developed a bundle of apps which allowed Ubuntu's staff to digitalise their services with the specific purpose of speeding up the processes belonging to each of their projects.
My personal contribution has been strong in the planning phase while my main task has been taking care of the technical aspects of each app. This project introced me to FileMaker, a software in which I greatly improved my comprehension during the year.
The experience earned with this tool makes me feel totally confident with it.

PharmAdi

PharmAdi is a personal project on which I am currently working on.
It has been created during the Covid-19 emergency to support the Home Care Service of the San Severo's Hospital. The problem they are facing is the huge flow of patients they need to take care of, with special focus on the management of medical supplies provided by the hospital to the nurses that perform the home service.
Our solution is the development of an application that can bring a benefit in the optimisation of these resources by reducing the time of the personnel involved in performing long and complex calculation tasks. I have been investigating the details of the process by personally going on the field while my current technical task is to take care of the User Interface and User Experience of the product.
We are in contact with Claris Int. to get support for the development of this project along side with FileMaker.
